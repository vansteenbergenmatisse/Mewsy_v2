# How to retrieve the 'Grant Token' from Visma e-Conomics

This article outlines the steps to provide Omniboost with a Visma e-Economic agreement grant token. This token enables access to an e-conomic administration and facilitates the integration setup.

---

## Visit the Omniboost App Page for Visma e-Conomics

The first step is to visit the 'Omniboost App Page,' please follow this link to begin the process:

https://secure.e-conomic.com/secure/api1/requestaccess.aspx?appPublicToken=BfpD17m2hscoMa640mgfgi3cuUIqJexaT4Jqeba1CNo1&redirectUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fomniboost.io (https://secure.e-conomic.com/secure/api1/requestaccess.aspx?appPublicToken=BfpD17m2hscoMa640mgfgi3cuUIqJexaT4Jqeba1CNo1&redirectUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fomniboost.io)

Once on the selected page, log in with your Visma e-Conomics credentials and proceed to the next step(s).

**Note**: For a visual guide, please refer to the image below for further information.

## Authorize the Omniboost App

Once logged in, you will see a screen to add the 'Omniboost App' to your Visma Agreement. Please read the information carefully, then press 'Add app'.

**Note**: For a visual guide, please refer to the image below for further information.

## Retrieve the 'Grant Token'

Once you click 'Add App,' copy the provided token and forward the token to your designated Omniboost Agent handling the onboarding so we can establish a connection to Visma e-Conomic.
