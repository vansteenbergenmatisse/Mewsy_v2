## What Omniboost Is

Omniboost is a **Data Unification Platform** for the hospitality industry. Their tagline is *"The Unification Platform that Enables them all."* They are the global leader in hospitality data unification and automation, headquartered in the Netherlands. Their core mission is to break down data silos by connecting all fragmented hospitality systems — POS, PMS, accounting, and more — into a single automated data flow, turning complexity into clarity.

## Core Products

Omniboost offers five main products:

- **Accounting** — Connects PMS/POS systems to accounting software, automating financial data flows and eliminating manual journal entries.
- **PMS (Property Management System)** — Integrations that use a PMS as the central hub for operational and financial data.
- **POS (Point of Sale)** — Connects POS systems to PMS and/or accounting, enabling real-time room charges and revenue synchronization.
- **Insights** — Data warehousing, dashboards, and reporting solutions. Provides KPIs like ADR, RevPAR, GOP PAR, CoGS, and more across all connected systems.
- **Owners Portal** — Works on top of a PMS to give property owners real-time visibility into availability, reservations, revenue, and financials. Fully customizable with per-user access control.

## How Omniboost Works

Omniboost sits as a **connectivity layer** between hospitality software systems. It pulls data from a source system (e.g. a PMS or POS), transforms and maps it, and pushes it to a destination system (e.g. an accounting platform) in real-time or on a scheduled basis. The platform uses AI and machine learning to ensure seamless connectivity, self-onboarding, error management, and self-help tooling. Over 450 software configurations are supported and counting.
