## Mews is a hotel management system

Mews is a cloud-based Property Management System (PMS). A PMS is the software a hotel uses to manage everything: taking bookings, checking guests in and out, tracking which rooms are occupied, handling payments, running the restaurant or bar, and generating financial reports. Mews powers over 15,000 hotels across 85 countries and has been voted Best PMS in 2024, 2025, and 2026 by Hotel Tech Report.

## What Mews tracks for accounting

Every time a guest consumes something at your hotel, a room night, a breakfast, a spa treatment, a drink at the bar, Mews records it. It also records every payment that comes in (cash, card, online, city ledger, etc.). All of this gets organised into the Mews Accounting Report, which is what Omniboost reads every day to build your journal entries.

## Omniboost's role with Mews

Omniboost is the primary integration partner for Mews. Almost all the accounting integrations you see listed in the Mews Marketplace are built and managed by Omniboost. Omniboost's job is to move your data from Mews to your accounting system. It does not manage your Mews setup itself. Any changes needed inside Mews must be made by the hotel directly.

## How to find an Omniboost integration in Mews

In the Mews Marketplace, look at the description beneath a connection name. If it says Omniboost, that integration is managed by Omniboost. To start one: log in to Mews, go to Marketplace, then Accounting, find your accounting system, click Explore, and request the connection. Omniboost will then send you an onboarding email.

## One critical setting: turn off Receivable Tracking

Before you activate any Omniboost integration, you must turn OFF Receivable Tracking in Mews. If it is left on, both Mews and Omniboost will try to create invoices, and you will end up with duplicates in your accounting system. Where to find it: Mews Menu, then Settings, then Property, then Finance, then Accounting Configuration, then turn off Receivable Tracking.
