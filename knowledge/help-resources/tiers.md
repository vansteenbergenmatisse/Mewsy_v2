## There are three tiers

Omniboost offers three integration tiers for the Mews accounting integration: Bronze, Silver, and Gold. The right tier depends on your property's complexity, how much detail you need in your accounting reports, how much support you want during setup, and your budget.

## Bronze: free (included in Mews)

Bronze is included in your Mews subscription at no extra cost. It covers the basics: revenue, payments, VAT, and accounts receivable, all transferred at the aggregated Mews Accounting Category level. Support is handled by Mews, not Omniboost directly. You validate the test journals yourself using the Omniboost Help Center. Best for properties that want basic automation and are comfortable with self-service.

## Bronze does not include

- Detailed (non-aggregated) revenue or payment breakdowns
- The ability to skip or exclude specific categories
- Credit card fee splitting
- Statistics entries (arrivals, departures, etc.)
- Custom journal or line descriptions
- Market segmentation for accommodation revenues
- Direct Omniboost email or phone support
- Post-go-live reconciliation assistance from Omniboost
- Historical re-send beyond 30 days from go-live

## Silver: 1,600 EUR / GBP per year per property

Silver gives you everything in Bronze plus more detailed data and direct Omniboost support. You get granular revenue and payment breakdowns (not just totals by category), the ability to skip specific categories entirely, credit card fee splitting (gross payment split into net plus commission), and Omniboost assists you through the test journal validation. Support SLA: within 3 business days. Historical re-send: up to 90 days.

## Silver does not include (compared to Gold)

- Statistics entries (arrivals, departures, rooms out of order, etc.)
- Custom journal descriptions
- Custom revenue and payment line descriptions
- Market segmentation for accommodation revenues
- Advanced mapping logic
- Monthly meetings (only quarterly)
- 90-day reconciliation assistance (only 30 days)
- Priority 1-business-day response (only 3 business days)

## Gold: 2,150 EUR / GBP per year per property

Gold is the full package. Everything in Silver, plus: statistics entries, market segmentation for accommodation (OTA, Leisure, Business, Transient, etc.), advanced mapping logic for complex setups, fully customisable journal and line descriptions, monthly meetings, 90-day reconciliation assistance, and the fastest support SLA of within 1 business day. Best for complex hotels, multi-property groups, or any property that needs maximum control and customisation.

## How to decide

- Bronze: if you want basic automation at no extra cost and are happy to support yourself
- Silver: if you need detailed data, want Omniboost to handle validation and support, or need credit card fee splitting
- Gold: if you have complex mapping needs, need statistics or market segmentation, run multiple properties, want full description customisation, or need the fastest support SLA
