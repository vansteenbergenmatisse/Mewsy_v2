## The big idea

Omniboost is a Data Unification Platform for the hospitality industry. Think of it as a translator that sits between your hotel software and your accounting software. It reads the data from one system, understands it, and writes it into the other automatically. Without Omniboost, someone at your hotel would have to manually copy numbers from one system to the other every single day. With Omniboost, that just happens by itself.

## What Omniboost connects

Omniboost connects over 450 software configurations across hotels, restaurants, and other hospitality businesses. It can link your Property Management System (PMS), Point of Sale (POS), accounting software, revenue management tools, and more into one automated data flow.

## The five Omniboost products

- Accounting: automatically sends your daily revenue and payment data from your PMS or POS straight into your accounting software. No more manual journal entries.
- PMS: uses your Property Management System as the central hub that all other systems talk to.
- POS: connects your restaurant or bar system to both your PMS and accounting software, so room charges and revenue sync in real time.
- Insights: turns your data into dashboards and reports with KPIs like ADR, RevPAR, GOP, and cost of goods sold, pulled automatically from all your connected systems.
- Owners Portal: gives property owners a real-time view of availability, reservations, revenue, and financials, with per-user access control.

## Why it matters

Every time a person manually copies a number from one system to another, there is a chance for a mistake. Omniboost removes that risk entirely. It also means your accountant gets the numbers automatically every morning instead of waiting for someone to send a file. That is time saved, errors avoided, and better financial data quality across the board.
