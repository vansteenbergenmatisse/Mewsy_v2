## Before you begin: one critical step

Turn OFF Receivable Tracking in Mews before doing anything else. Location: Mews Menu, then Settings, then Property, then Finance, then Accounting Configuration. If this is left on and you activate the Omniboost integration, you will get duplicate invoices in your accounting system. This must be done first, no exceptions.

## Step 1: request the connection in Mews

Log in to Mews, go to Marketplace, then Accounting, find your accounting system, click Explore, and click the button to request the connection. Omniboost will receive the request and send an onboarding invitation email to the email address on the Mews profile you used.

## Step 2: complete the Omniboost onboarding wizard

Follow the onboarding wizard in the Omniboost portal. You will go through: selecting your accounting flow (Consumed or Closed), entering your company and contact details, accepting the terms and conditions, connecting your Mews token, connecting your accounting system, and completing your GL mapping.

## Step 3: gather what you need for GL mapping

- An export of your Chart of Accounts from your accounting system
- VAT / tax codes from your accounting platform
- Your general journal code and sales journal code
- Ledger account codes for fallback revenue and fallback payments
- The ledger code for your guest ledger and city ledger (if applicable)
- The ledger code for any gateway suspense accounts
- Whether you want to book gateway commission costs (if yes, provide the ledger code)
- Your preferred end of day (default is midnight, contact Omniboost to change it)

## Step 4: test period

Omniboost will run a test period where journal entries are sent to your accounting system but should not be finalised yet. Bronze tier: 7 days, you validate yourself. Silver tier: 21 days, Omniboost helps validate. Gold tier: 1 month, Omniboost assists with full validation. Review the test journals carefully against your Mews Accounting Report before going live.

## Step 5: go live

Once test journals are validated and correct, Omniboost activates the full automation. From that point on, the previous day's data will be posted to your accounting system automatically every morning. You will also be able to trigger manual pushes for any date range from the Omniboost portal.

## Who does what?

Omniboost performs a final setup check but does not modify your Mews environment themselves. Any recommended changes inside Mews must be carried out by the hotel. Omniboost is also not responsible for generating your chart-of-accounts codes, those come from your accounting system.
